About Paws & Whiskers

Your trusted partner in pet care since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Sarah and Michael Johnson, Paws & Whiskers began as a small family-owned pet store with a big dream: to provide the highest quality care and products for pets and their owners.

What started as a modest shop has grown into a comprehensive pet care destination, serving thousands of happy pets and their families. Our commitment to excellence and love for animals has remained unchanged throughout our journey.

Today, we're proud to offer a full range of services including grooming, veterinary care, training, and boarding, all under one roof.
