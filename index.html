Founded in 2010, Paws & Whiskers has been serving the pet community with love, care, and the highest quality products and services. We believe every pet deserves the best care possible.

Our team of certified professionals is passionate about animals and committed to providing exceptional service to both pets and their owners.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pet care services

We offer a comprehensive range of pet care services including professional grooming, veterinary care, training sessions, boarding facilities, and a wide selection of pet supplies and accessories. Our experienced team ensures your pets receive the best care possible.

While we do accept walk-ins when possible, we highly recommend booking appointments in advance to ensure availability and reduce wait times. You can book appointments online, by phone, or in person at our location.

We're open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sundays from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Our extended hours ensure you can visit us at your convenience, even after work or on weekends.

We provide basic veterinary care during our regular hours. For emergency situations outside of our hours, we recommend contacting your local emergency veterinary clinic. We can also provide referrals to trusted emergency care providers.
